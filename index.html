Language science for better AI.

Large Language Models are transforming how software is developed. Optimising performance, however, requires that developers carefully consider the language coming in and out of these systems during training and evaluation.

Although base models are powerful, performance on most tasks is improved by further pre-training on language from the target domain. But how should developers design and build these domain corpora?

The language output generated by applications based on Large Language Models is highly fluent. But how can the quality of this language be evaluated objectively to maximise communicative efficacy of these systems?

Lingsight is here to answer questions like these for software developers. We specialise in using advanced linguistic methods to guide model pre-training and assess generative outputs. Drawing on deep expertise in quantitative corpus linguistics, sociolinguistics, and cognitive linguistics, we help our clients ensure their AI systems are grounded in real patterns of human communication, designed for specific domains, tasks, styles, and audiences.
